MALLU TRUCKING CO.LTD

Mallu Trucking CO LTD (MTC) is a virtual trucking company in TruckersMP, the Multiplayer port for Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator 2. MTC was created in 13th June 2022, by some close trucking friends and now have grown into a bigger group than it previously was. The main function of MTC is virtual trucking with similar minded people. But the aim is not only limited to trucking, but MTC is also about friendship, co-operation, and also about having a good time with together, away from all the stresses of real world. We support each other and we love trucking, a match made in heaven. Even if the initial plan of MTC was just a group of friends to chill and drive together, more like-minded people joined our community and we are growing now! The core of the group is togetherness, and politeness. And our weekly / monthly events are aimed towards this goal of togetherness and having a good time! Come ride with us!
