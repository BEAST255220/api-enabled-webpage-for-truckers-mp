About us

Mallu Trucking Co Ltd (MTC) is the first virtual trucking company in Kerala, setting a pioneering example in the region. Established on 13th June 2022 by a group of close friends, MTC has evolved into a larger community, attracting like-minded individuals who share a passion for virtual trucking. While the primary focus of MTC is on providing a platform for enjoyable trucking experiences, it goes beyond that. MTC emphasizes friendship, cooperation, and creating a stress-free environment away from the real world. The company values togetherness and politeness, fostering a welcoming and respectful community. Members of MTC support and uplift each other, forming strong bonds based on their shared love for trucking. The company organizes regular weekly and monthly events to promote unity and provide enjoyable experiences for all members. MTC extends an invitation to others to join their community and embark on a fantastic journey in the virtual trucking world.
