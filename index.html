MALLU TRUCKING CO.LTD

Mallu Trucking Company is a unique gaming community that caters to trucking enthusiasts who love playing simulation games like Euro Truck Simulator and American Truck Simulator. Our community is made up of individuals who share a passion for trucking and who come together to engage in a variety of virtual trucking adventures. At Mallu Trucking Company, we are dedicated to creating a welcoming and inclusive community where trucking enthusiasts can come together to share their love of the open road. Our members hail from all across the globe but share a common bond in their love for all things trucking. Whether you are a seasoned trucking veteran or just starting out, Mallu Trucking Company is the perfect place to connect with like-minded individuals and engage in immersive and exciting virtual trucking experiences. So come join us on the road and experience the thrill of trucking like never before!
